UI/UX Design — Complete Guide

Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced path with tools, projects & portfolios

Overview

UI/UX designers create visually appealing and user-friendly digital products focusing on usability, accessibility, and design aesthetics.

Beginner (Foundations)

  • 🎨 Design Principles: color theory, typography, layout, spacing
  • 📐 Wireframing & Prototyping: Figma, Adobe XD basics
  • 🖥 User Research: surveys, interviews, personas
  • Projects: Simple landing page UI, wireframe for app

Intermediate (Interaction & Tools)

  • 🔄 Interaction Design: micro-interactions, animations
  • 📊 UX Process: user flows, journey mapping, usability testing
  • 🛠 Tools: Figma advanced, Sketch, Adobe XD, InVision
  • Projects: Design full app screens with interactive prototype

Advanced (Professional & Portfolio)

  • 🖌 Advanced Visual Design: responsive design, design systems
  • 💡 UX Strategy: accessibility, A/B testing, analytics integration
  • 🗂 Portfolio: case studies, showcase design workflow
  • Certifications: NN/g UX Certification, Google UX Design
  • Projects: End-to-end product design with usability testing

Recommended Tools & Resources

  • Docs: Material Design Guidelines, Apple HIG, NN/g articles
  • Free learning: YouTube tutorials, Figma community, freeCodeCamp UI/UX
  • Courses: Udemy UI/UX bootcamps, Coursera Google UX Design
  • Practice: Redesign existing apps, build case studies

Roadmap Actions (Weekly Plan)

  • Weeks 1–3: Learn design principles & wireframing
  • Weeks 4–6: Prototyping, interaction design, UX research
  • Weeks 7–10: Full product design, usability testing, portfolio start
  • Weeks 11–14: Advanced portfolio, case studies, certifications
